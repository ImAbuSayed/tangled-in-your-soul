[Hook]
Shadows falling soft across the wooden floor
Every look you give is making me want more
Lost inside the rhythm of your quiet breathing
You're the only reason that my heart is beating

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers in the autumn breeze
But in this darkened room, you bring me to my knees
Your silhouette is painted by the midnight moon
We are drinking down the shadows in this quiet room

[Pre-Chorus]
Slowly pulling closer as the fire burns down
We're the only two awake in this sleepy town

[Chorus]

There is nowhere in this world I'd ever rather be
